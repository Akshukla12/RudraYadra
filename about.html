RUDRA YATRA

The Two Souls Behind This Journey

Ram Raj Arya

Founder & Guardian of the Source

Stationed in the Arun Valley itself, Ram Raj Arya lives close to the land and close to Shiva. He selects each Rudraksha personally — reading its mukhis, sensing its energy, ensuring not a single bead leaves the forest without being worthy of the seeker it will serve.

He brings decades of spiritual knowledge and an unshakeable bhav (devotion) to every piece he touches.

Anvi Raj

Co-founder & The Bridge Between Two Worlds

Anvi Raj carries what Ram Raj Arya gathers — not just the Rudraksha, but the intention, the purity, and the guidance that comes with it — from Nepal into India, and into the hands of each seeker.

She ensures that what reaches you is exactly what left the source: untouched, unaltered, complete. She also serves as your first point of connection — understanding your needs, your challenges, and helping you find the Rudraksha that is truly meant for you.

हमारा संकल्प

Our Promise

हम मानते हैं कि जब रुद्राक्ष केवल एक व्यावसायिक वस्तु बन जाता है, तो उसकी पवित्रता कहीं न कहीं कम हो जाती है। इसलिए हमने शिव और आपसे कुछ संकल्प किए हैं।

We believe that the moment Rudraksha becomes a commercial product, it loses something sacred. So we have made certain promises — to Shiva, and to you.

हम क्या नहीं करते What We Never Do

No certificates or lab testing
No machine drilling or polishing
No factory processing
No packaging-first thinking
No false grading or upselling

हम क्या करते हैं What We Always Do

Full spiritual guidance on selection
Hand-examined, naturally sourced beads
Direct from source trees in Arun Valley
Vidhis and wearing instructions with every bead
Honest, need-based Rudraksha matching

What You Actually Receive

When you come to us, you do not simply receive a Rudraksha. You receive:

The bead chosen for your specific need or dosha
The correct vidhi (ritual method) for wearing it
Guidance on mantras, timing, and intention
The knowledge to make this a living spiritual practice — not a purchase
We do not believe in "buy and forget." We believe in dharan karna — wearing with awareness, with faith, with Shiva.
