How to Choose
Your Bead

Finding the exact frequency that your soul requires.

Choosing a Rudraksha is not about "what looks good" or simply buying the most expensive bead. It is about matching the electromagnetic frequency of the bead (determined by its Mukhi/faces) to the energetic gaps in your own life and astrological chart.

Method 1: Choosing by Current Life Challenges (Intent)

The most ancient and practical way to choose is based on the specific hurdles you are facing right now.

  • Mental Turmoil & Anxiety: The 5 Mukhi is the universal pacifier, providing extreme calm.
  • Financial Struggles & Debt: The 7 Mukhi dissolves blockages to wealth and protects against losses.
  • Fear, Timidity & Low Confidence: The 3 Mukhi ignites inner fire and the 9 Mukhi offers ultimate fearlessness.
  • Career Stagnation: The 6 Mukhi connects to Lord Kartikeya, offering warrior-like grounding and promotion.
  • Seeking Moksha / Spiritual Depth: The 1 Mukhi or 14 Mukhi handles extreme spiritual acceleration.

Method 2: Choosing by Vedic Astrology (Kundli)

Every Mukhi represents a specific celestial body. By identifying which planets are heavily afflicted (weak or negative) in your Kundli, you can prescribe a Rudraksha to drastically pacify that malefic effect.

  • Sun (Surya) Afflicted: Cured by 1 Mukhi or 12 Mukhi.
  • Moon (Chandra) Afflicted: Cured by 2 Mukhi.
  • Saturn (Shani) / Sade Sati: Cured heavily by 7 Mukhi or 14 Mukhi.
  • Rahu & Ketu Doshas: Cured by 8 Mukhi (Rahu) and 9 Mukhi (Ketu).

Method 3: The Universal Combinations

If you do not have your birth chart and are unsure of your core challenges, the Vedic sages recommend standard harmonious combinations:

  • Saraswati Bandh (4, 6 Mukhi): Perfect for students, scholars, and speakers.
  • Swasthya Bandh (3, 5 Mukhi): Excellent for general physical and mental wellbeing.
  • Siddha Mala (1 to 14 Mukhi): Worn by advanced seekers, it contains all energies and pacifies all planets simultaneously.
