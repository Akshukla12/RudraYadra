आन्तरिक अग्नि का मनका The Bead of Inner Fire

The 3 Mukhi Rudraksha is governed by Agni Deva (the Fire God) and represents the life-giving energy of the sun. It is a powerful tool for purification, burning away the weight of past karmas and deep-rooted guilt.

It is a highly active bead that shatters inferiority complexes, removes deep-seated shame, and instills the wearer with intense courage and self-confidence. Ideal for those who feel stuck or held back by past mistakes.

महत्वपूर्ण दिशा-निर्देश Important Guidance

Authentic sacred guidance means telling you what aligns — and what does not. Please read before proceeding.

Wearing Rules

  • Metal & Contact: Wear in copper or silver wire, touching the skin at chest level.
  • Purity & Taboos: Remove during intimacy, funerals, or consuming non-veg food.
  • Activation: Chant "ॐ नमः शिवाय" 108 times on a Monday morning.
  • Reverence: Remove only for activation; handle with clean, reverent intent.

Consult Before Wearing

  • Anger & Aggression: Prone to temper issues (this bead increases fiery energy).
  • Children: Below 12 years, unless prescribed for specific health issues.
