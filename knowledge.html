Explore The Mukhi

Eka Mukhi

1 Mukhi

Lord Shiva

The rarest of all beads. It symbolizes pure consciousness and provides the wearer with the power of focused meditation and ultimate liberation.

Dvi Mukhi

2 Mukhi

Ardhanarishvara

Represents the union of Shiva and Shakti. It brings harmony to relationships and balances the dualities of the inner and outer self.

Pancha Mukhi

5 Mukhi

Kalagni Rudra

The most common yet powerful bead. It purifies the five elements within the body and grants peace of mind and mental clarity.

Sapta Mukhi

7 Mukhi

Goddess Lakshmi

A bead of prosperity and grace. It is said to invite financial abundance and dissolve the obstacles that block one's worldly success.

Ekadasha Mukhi

11 Mukhi

Lord Hanuman

Bestows the wearer with supreme courage, physical strength, and wisdom. It is the protector against negative influences.

Ekavimshati Mukhi

21 Mukhi

Lord Kubera

The pinnacle of abundance. This sacred bead represents Lord Kubera, the treasurer of gods, granting immense wealth and preservation of dharma.
